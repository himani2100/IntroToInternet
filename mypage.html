Throne of Glass series

I am going to use the content section to explain about the photo. The photo is a quote from the Throne of glass book series by Sarah Maas . The book series revolves around an imprisoned assassin who is offered her freedom back if she were to win a competition and that sets off a series of events of unescapable entwining fates. She rediscovers her powers and realizes she is the last hope of liberation to the people of a fallen kingdom. The book follows the journey of Celaena Sardothien from being an infamous assassin to her embracing her right to the throne of the country of Terassen and becoming Queen Aelin Ashryver Whitethorn Galathynius . I really like this book series because it has an unconventional and strong female lead and depicts depression realistically in a person.
